WHAT THIS PORTFOLIO IS ALL ABOUT?

Data is often seen as a necessary evil in today's world, which when utilized smartly and responsibly, it becomes a powerful tool for informed decision-making. In this portfolio, I demonstrate my ability to extract valuable insights and make data-driven decisions through diverse projects. My work explains the following aspects of a Data Analysis/Data Science Project:

  • Data Preparation and Data Cleaning
  • Exploratory Data Analaysis (EDA)
  • Feature Engineering
  • Model Selection and Training
  • Model Evaluation and Validation
  • Results Interpretation and Visualization

By exploring my portfolio, you'll witness how data analysis and data science can solve complex problems and drive meaningful outcomes, benefiting both individuals and businesses alike.

Titanic Survival Prediction

On April 15, 1912, during her maiden voyage, RMS Titanic sank after colliding with an iceberg. This project aims to build a predictive model that answers the question: “What sorts of people were more likely to survive?” using passenger data (name, age, gender, socio-economic class, etc).
